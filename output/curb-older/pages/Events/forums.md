## Annual Spring and Fall Research Forum

The Cornell Undergraduate Research Board (CURB) is proud to host the 30th Annual Undergraduate Spring Research Forum. The Annual Undergraduate Spring Research Forum is the largest undergraduate research forum held at Cornell with over a hundred undergraduates presenters in all fields of research (physics, philosophy, art history, biological engineering, etc.) and over 700 attendees (including undergraduate and graduate students, faculty, staff members, and others).

Event Details:

What : CURB's 30th Annual Spring Research Forum  
Where: Physical Science Building Atrium  
When : Wednesday, April 22nd, 2015 from 5:30PM to 7:00PM  


![Call for Abstracts](http://courses2.cit.cornell.edu/CURB/images/photos/Call_For_Abstracts_icon.jpg)

## Humanities Showcase

Come share your creative ideas, discoveries, and research developments you have been working on at Cornell! This is a great opportunity to inform the academic community about your own research, as well as learn about multifaceted research that occurs on campus. If you are passionate about your work, submit a description for your project today. Influence and inspire others! 


Event Details:

What : CURB's 2nd Annual Undergraduate Humanities Showcase  
Where: Johnson Art Museum  
When : Wednesday, April 23rd, 2014 from 5:00PM to 7:00PM  
Format: ~10-minute oral presentations, opportunities for other presentation styles*  

*This event is not limited to humanities work. If you have any questions or comments, do not hesitate to email curb@cornell.edu or visit the CURB web page at www.geo.cornell.edu/CURB/ We look forward to seeing your presentation!
