CURB PMP Spring 2016 Mentor Application

Program Details/Mentor Responsibilities:

  • Lead and advise interested peer groups on how to get involved with undergraduate research in your fields of study.
  • Assist in organizing research seminars, presentations, lab tours (required), and writing workshops.
  • Lead discussions and participate in research events.
  • Develop relationships and connections with other undergraduate students and professors in the research community.
  • Be assigned mentees; within each group, each mentor will be responsible for ~4-6 students
  • Lead a tour of your lab, with permission from your professor
  • Provide advice to your mentees on a consistent basis

The expected time commitment for a mentor is on average 6 hours per month.

Note: You must have at least one semester or one summer of research experience to be eligible to apply to be a CURB Peer Mentor.

The deadline for the application is February 7th at 11:59PM!
