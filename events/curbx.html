CURBx

CURB is excited to announce its second annual CURBx which will be held on November 21, 2016! Similar to TEDx Talks, come hear undergraduate students talk about their experiments and research. The talks will cover a variety of topics from the humanities and STEM fields. There will be awards and prizes given out. You won't want to miss this opportunity! Location to be announced.

If you are interested in presenting or have any questions about the event, please email an434@cornell.edu.
