About Us

We are the Cornell Undergraduate Research Board, and our mission is to connect you with exciting research opportunities and people.

Forums, from the Fall and Spring research forums to the Humanities Showcase, combine campus-wide poster sessions and research keynotes.

Our Peer Mentorship Program helps you map out your research path with a mentor.

Our Dinner Series brings together students and faculty to share a meal and talk science.


Join our mailing list to stay up to date on our upcoming events as well as the newest research scholarships and positions.
