Join CURB's E-Board!

Interested in gaining leadership experience while contributing to the premier research group on campus? We welcome you to apply to the CURB executive board for the 2016-17 season. The CURB E-board is responsible for creating programs and social opportunities for student researchers on campus.

If you're interested in joining CURB's executive board, fill out the following application and email it to our recruitment chair, Victoria Lazarov, at vkl9@cornell.edu by Tuesday, September 13th.
